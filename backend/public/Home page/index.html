About Our Institution

Dedicated to nurturing talent and fostering academic brilliance in rural Karnataka.

A Legacy of Excellence For Over 4 Decades

S.V.E. Society’s B. R. Darur First Grade College, Harugeri started functioning from 1983. The institution is committed to offering quality service to all its stakeholders under the visionary leadership of Shri. B.R. Darur.

Our college is endowed with devoted teaching staff possessing M.Phil and Ph.D degrees. We are proud to be Reaccredited with ‘A’ Grade (3.05 CGPA) by NAAC in 2021.
