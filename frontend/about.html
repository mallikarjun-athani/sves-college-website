Our History

Shri Vrushabhendra Education Society (SVES) was founded in 1983 with a noble mission to bring quality education to the rural heartlands of Karnataka. Over the years, the institution has grown from a small educational initiative into a prestigious center for academic excellence.

Our institution is reaccredited with an 'A' Grade by NAAC, reflecting our persistent commitment to maintaining the highest standards of teaching, research, and infrastructure.

  • 1983
    Establishment of SVES Society by visionary founders.
  • 1995
    Introduction of Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA).
  • 2015
    NAAC 'A' Grade Accreditation received.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution of higher learning, transforming rural youth into globally competitive and morally upright citizens through quality education.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality education while fostering holistic development through professional training and community engagement.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Social Responsibility, and Continuous Innovation in all our academic and administrative pursuits.
