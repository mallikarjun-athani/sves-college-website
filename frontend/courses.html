Undergraduate Programs

Shape your career with our diverse range of academic courses designed for the modern world.

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

Duration: 3 Years Eligibility: PU-II / 10+2

Focuses on computer science fundamentals, software development, web technologies, and database management. Includes practical training in modern programming languages.

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

Duration: 3 Years Eligibility: PU-II / 10+2

A comprehensive program covering accounting, finance, taxation, business law, and management. Prepares students for roles in banking, insurance, and corporate sectors.

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc)

Duration: 3 Years Eligibility: PU-II Science

Offers specializations in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Computer Science. Emphasizes experimental learning and scientific inquiry.

Bachelor of Arts (BA)

Duration: 3 Years Eligibility: PU-II / 10+2

Focuses on humanities, languages, and social sciences. Encourages critical thinking and deep understanding of human society and culture.
